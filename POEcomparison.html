Quick Positioning Guide

Choose C-Series When:

  • Simple PoE switching
  • The network does not require VLAN tagging, LACP, or security policies
  • Budget is a priority
  • Deployment is single-site and straightforward

Choose L2-Series When:

  • You need advanced L2+ features (VLAN, STP, LACP)
  • Security requirements are strict
  • You manage large-scale camera networks
  • The project includes multiple subnets or redundant architecture
  • IPv6 is required
Sales Message You Can Use:
“C-Series is designed for simple and cost-effective PoE deployments, while the L2-Series delivers enterprise-class management, security, and scalability for professional surveillance networks.”
